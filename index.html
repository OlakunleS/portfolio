About Me

My name is Olakunle. I am an American Graphic Designer and web designer. I have lived in Memphis, Tennessee my whole life. Growing up, my favorite hobby was playing games and working on designs. Either playing on a PlayStation or building structures with K’nex pieces. I became interested in Multimedia. However, my school didn’t have it as a major, so I chose business. I worked hard in the States, so I could study what I loved in Canada. This website is my portfolio and will be used for business inquiries.
